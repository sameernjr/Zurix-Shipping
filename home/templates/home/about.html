FAQs

What Shipping Methods do you offer ?

We offer Standard Shipping (3-5 business days) and Expedited Shipping (1-2 business days) within Malaysia.

Do you ship internationally ?

Yes, we ship to most countries. International shipping rates vary by destination and will be calculated at checkout.

What are your estimanted delivery times ?

Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days within the Malaysia.
Expedited Shipping: 1-2 business days within the Malaysia.
International Shipping: 7-14 business days, but may vary depending on the destination and customs clearance.

How can I track my order ?

Go to contact us page and request your package to be tracked, or you can calll our phone line and send us an email with your request.
